# Privacy Policy

**Operator:** Tao Yan, trading as Zero Carbon Logic

**Last updated:** June 25, 2026

## Data We Collect

- **License key hash** — A one-way hash stored locally (SQLite) to enforce usage limits. The original key is not stored.
- **Anonymized query terms** — Processed in-memory for factor matching, not persisted beyond the active request.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- **LLM context or prompts** — Your AI agent's context is processed ephemerally and never logged.
- **Personal credentials** — No names, emails, or PII are collected through the MCP service.
- **Payment information** — All payments handled by [Paddle](https://www.paddle.com). We never see your card details.

## Data Usage

- License key hashes are used solely for usage tracking and rate limiting.
- No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

- **Paddle** — Payment processing. See [Paddle Privacy Policy](https://www.paddle.com/legal/privacy).
- **Cloudflare** — Hosting and CDN. See [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Your Rights

Request data deletion at nikeandocean@gmail.com. We respond within 30 days.
