**Operator:** Zero Carbon Logic

**Last updated:** June 25, 2026

## 14-Day Refund Guarantee

**We offer a 14-day unconditional refund policy** for our Pro license keys, in full compliance with Paddle's Buyer Terms.

## Eligibility

You may request a full refund within **14 days** of your purchase date, no questions asked.

## How to Request

Contact us at **nikeandocean@gmail.com** with subject line "Refund Request — [Your Order ID]". Please include your Paddle order ID from your purchase confirmation email.

## Processing

- Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of approval.
- Refunds are credited back to the original payment method.
- Once a refund is issued, the associated license key will be deactivated.

## Exceptions

Requests after the 14-day window may be considered on a case-by-case basis for technical issues.
