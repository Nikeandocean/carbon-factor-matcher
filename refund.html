Refund Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

We offer a 14-day unconditional refund policy for our Pro license keys, in full compliance with Paddle’s Buyer Terms.

1. Refund Eligibility

You may request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase date, no questions asked. This policy applies to all Pro license key purchases made through our Paddle checkout.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with your order details:

  • Email: nikeandocean@gmail.com
  • Subject line: “Refund Request — [Your Order ID]”

Please include your Paddle order ID (found in your purchase confirmation email) so we can process your request promptly.

3. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of approval.
  • The refund will be credited back to the original payment method used at checkout.
  • Once a refund is issued, the associated license key will be deactivated immediately.

4. Exceptions

Refund requests made after the 14-day window may be considered on a case-by-case basis for technical issues that prevent the service from functioning as described.

5. Contact

For any questions about our refund policy, contact us at nikeandocean@gmail.com.
